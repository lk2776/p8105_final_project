Brief Introduction of our project

Dairy Industry Insights Project Our project delves into the dairy industry, focusing on:

  • Milking Cows and Production: We compile data on milk production and cow numbers across various states.
  • Different Milk Types: We analyze the production dynamics of whole milk, 2%, 1%, and skim milk, highlighting trends and influencing factors.
  • Nutritional Analysis: We examine the nutritional content of various milk types, including fat, protein, vitamins, and minerals, offering insights into their health benefits and dietary impacts.
